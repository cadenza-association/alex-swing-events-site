1. Description of Service

Alex Swing Events Publications is a web-based social media management application that enables live music event organizers and promoters to:

  • Connect their social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube) via official OAuth 2.0 authorization

The App publishes content exclusively to accounts that have explicitly authorized the App through the respective platform's official authorization flow.
