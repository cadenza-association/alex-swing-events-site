This Privacy Policy explains how Alex Swing Events Publications ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our social media management tool.

2. What Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Follower data, audience data, or any data about your TikTok followers
  • Analytics or engagement metrics from your TikTok account
  • Personal information about TikTok users who interact with your content
  • Payment information
  • Location data
  • Device fingerprinting or behavioral tracking data

3. How We Use TikTok Data

Data obtained through TikTok API Services is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • user.info.basic: Verify which TikTok account has authorized the App. Display the account name for confirmation. This data is not stored beyond the authentication session.
  • video.publish: Submit video content to TikTok on behalf of the authenticated account owner. The App does not retain, analyze, or process video content after publication.

TikTok data is never used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than the publishing workflow described above.

Our use of TikTok API Services is subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your data or TikTok API data with any third parties, except:

  • TikTok: Video content and metadata are transmitted to TikTok's servers as part of the publication process
  • Cloudinary: Video files are temporarily hosted for the purpose of making them accessible to platform APIs; files may be deleted after publication
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or court order

5. Data Security

Access tokens and API credentials are stored as encrypted environment variables on secured servers (Render.com infrastructure). We apply industry-standard security practices to prevent unauthorized access.

6. Data Retention

  • Access tokens: retained until revoked by the user or until token expiration
  • Video files: deleted immediately after successful publication to the target platform
  • Account identifiers: not retained beyond the active session

7. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Revoke the App's access to your TikTok account via TikTok's app permissions settings
  • Request deletion of any data associated with your account by contacting us at the address below
  • Request information about what data we hold about you

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
